How it works

Four workflows. All live today.

Each workflow is a complete, end-to-end automation — from raw PDF in to finished documents out, with every step logged and every number reconciled.

01
Ingest the capital call PDF
Drop in the capital call notice from the underlying fund. HighTraverse extracts the fund name, total amount, and due date automatically — no data entry.
02
Map to your vehicle
The investment is matched to the correct fund-of-funds vehicle using your mapping table. Works across multiple vehicles and GP relationships.
03
Calculate pro-rata allocations
Every LP's share computed from their ownership percentage. Rounding handled per fund accounting standards — adjustment goes to the largest LP so the total always reconciles exactly.
04
Output to Excel + log everything
A formatted allocations spreadsheet is generated with a complete header block and LP-by-LP breakdown. Every run is appended to an audit log with timestamps.

01
Extract wire instructions from the notice
Bank name, ABA routing number, account number, and beneficiary name are extracted from the capital call PDF automatically — no copy-paste from the notice.
02
Compare against historical records
The extracted wire instructions are compared against the last known good record for that fund. This is the wire fraud detection layer — any change in routing number or account triggers a flag.
03
Status: NEW, MATCH, or CHANGED
Every wire template is stamped with a verification status. MATCH = green light. NEW = first-time, verify verbally. CHANGED = stop, do not wire — verbal confirmation required before proceeding.
04
Generate the wire template PDF
A complete wire template is output with originator details, beneficiary, payment amount, color-coded alert box, and sign-off lines for approval.

Ingest the distribution notice PDF

02
Calculate LP distributions
Each LP's pro-rata share of the distribution is calculated from their ownership percentage. Rounding reconciles to the exact total — same fund accounting standard as capital calls.
03
Generate distribution notices
A branded distribution notice is generated per LP — green color scheme signals money coming in, not going out. Each notice shows their specific amount, payment date, and fund details.
04
Summary spreadsheet + audit log
A complete distributions.xlsx is written alongside the per-LP notices. Every run is logged to the same audit trail as capital calls, with transaction type tagged for reconciliation.
